Here’s a little Q&A about me.

A: I love working on custom websites – starting with just a “get to know you” conversation, and turning a vision into an actual product is so satisfying. I also really enjoy planning fun out-of-the-box events!

My Github account has some of the work I’ve been tinkering with lately.
