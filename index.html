ABOUT

A couple of things about me:

  • My love of cheese knows no bounds (Ask me about my cheese baby story. Better yet, read about it on my embarrassingly old blog).
  • I'm freakishly organized. It does not run in the family.
  • I really enjoy learning new things. I’ve been in one class or another since preschool. Check out my progress (and detours) here.

From the outside, my path looks pretty cut and dry. Went to college for marketing, got a job in marketing, started a company in marketing…but there’s more to me than that. Yes, I went to college for marketing, but I also co-founded a patent illustration company (with my dad, my biggest supporter). From there I took a big leap, moved across the country, and began my first "real" job in marketing. I learned a great deal and was given many opportunities to develop in areas I had interest (thanks, non-profit world!), such as video production and event coordination. While there, I also continued my education by taking graphic design, programming, marketing, and social media classes. I even got certified in Culinary Arts. After 4 years, I was restless for new opportunities, so I left my job and traveled for awhile. Seeing different parts of the world awakened a new spirit inside me, and gave me the courage to start my company. Running my own company, and doing everything myself (no dad to rely on this time!), from finding clients, to managing expenses, taxes, and the rest, is a daunting, exhausting, rewarding experience. I worked on marketing strategy, graphic design, brochures, websites, social media, you name it. Now I'm starting down the path to web developer. Where has your road taken you?
